Stream Speed Training For osu!

This website(and it's functionality) was designed by me(Sukurima) for osu! players to track their progress at completing streams(sequenses of circle notes) which need a lot of speed of two fingers. I'll continue to add new functionalities to this project until I will consider the project concluded.
